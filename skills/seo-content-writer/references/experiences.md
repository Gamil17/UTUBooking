## Customer Journey Stories (anonymised — use for relatable content)

### Story 1: The First-Time Hajj Pilgrim from Riyadh
A Saudi family from Riyadh booking their first Hajj. Primary concern: finding a hotel
close enough for the grandmother to walk without difficulty. Used the proximity filter
to select a hotel 150m from Gate 79. Grandmother completed all Tawaf on foot.
**Use for:** Haram proximity filter content, Hajj hotel booking guides, accessibility content

### Story 2: The Ramadan Rush
A Pakistani family tried to book Ramadan Umrah three weeks before Ramadan — found
all Zone 1 and Zone 2 hotels sold out. Booked Zone 3 with shuttle service.
Lesson: book 4-6 months ahead for Ramadan. Price was 3x off-season rate.
**Use for:** Ramadan Umrah guides, early booking content, pricing guides

### Story 3: The Malaysian Group Booking
A 35-person Malaysian Tabung Haji group coordinating through a travel agent.
Needed halal-certified hotel with segregated facilities and group dining.
Used UTUBooking's group booking + halal filter to find Millennium Makkah.
**Use for:** Malaysian market content, group travel, halal hotel guides

### Story 4: The Turkish Business Traveler
Turkish entrepreneur combining a Riyadh business trip with a 3-day Umrah.
Needed: Riyadh hotel Mon-Wed → Makkah hotel Thu-Sat → return flight.
Booked all three segments on UTUBooking in one session.
**Use for:** Business + Umrah combination travel, Turkish market content

### Story 5: The British Muslim Family
British-Pakistani family from Birmingham. First concern: GBP pricing and
understanding what SAR prices meant in real terms. UTUBooking showed GBP equivalent.
Second concern: halal-certified hotel. Booked with confidence using halal filter.
**Use for:** UK market content, diaspora Muslim travel, GBP pricing content

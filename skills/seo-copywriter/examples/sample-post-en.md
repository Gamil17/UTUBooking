## What Hotels Are Closest to Masjid Al-Haram in Makkah?

The closest hotels to Masjid Al-Haram are inside the Abraj Al-Bait complex, directly adjacent to the mosque. The Fairmont Makkah Clock Royal Tower, Swissotel Makkah, and Hilton Suites Makkah are all within a 3-minute walk of Gate 79. Budget options within 500 metres include Makkah Millennium Hotel and Marriott Makkah Hotel.

**Abraj Al-Bait Zone (0-200 metres):** This is prime Makkah real estate. You see the Kaaba from some rooms. The air-conditioned walkways mean no exposure to outdoor heat. The tradeoff is price: rooms in this zone start from SAR 1,500 per night and can exceed SAR 4,000 during Ramadan and Hajj season.

Top properties here include:

**Fairmont Makkah Clock Royal Tower** — Directly connected to the mosque on Level 2. Known for exceptional service and rooftop Haram views. Rooms from SAR 2,200/night.

**Swissotel Makkah** — Unobstructed Kaaba-facing rooms available (request at booking). Halal dining on every floor. Rooms from SAR 1,800/night.

**Hilton Suites Makkah** — Best for families needing kitchenette suites for 2-3 week Hajj stays. Rooms from SAR 1,500/night.

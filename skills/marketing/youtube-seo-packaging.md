# YouTube SEO Packaging
Reference: skills/seo-content-writer/references/tone-of-voice.md

## Inputs Required
Ask if not provided: video topic, primary keyword, video length, primary CTA

## Title — Generate 3 Options

Formula options:
- How-to: 'How to [Specific Result] for Umrah ([Qualifier])'
- List: '[Number] Things Every [Audience] Should Know Before [Action]'
- Reveal: 'The Real [Topic] — What No One Tells You Before Umrah'

Rules for all titles:
- Primary keyword in first 3 words where possible
- Under 60 characters
- No colons unless splitting keyword from qualifier
- Parenthetical qualifiers: '(2026 Guide)', '(Step-by-Step)', '(First-Time Pilgrim)'
- No ALL CAPS except 1-2 words maximum

UTUBooking title bank (adapt per video):
- 'Hotels Near Masjid Al-Haram — What Zone 1 Really Means'
- 'How to Filter Hotels by Walking Time to the Haram Gate'
- 'Umrah Hotel Booking Guide 2026 (First-Time Pilgrim)'
- 'How to Book Hajj Hotels From the UK — Complete Guide'
- 'Ramadan Umrah Prices: When to Book and Why It Matters'
- 'Halal Hotel Certification — What UTUBooking Actually Checks'

## Description — 400-600 Words

Block 1 (100 words): What the video covers, who it's for, and the main promise.
Include primary keyword naturally in the first 2 sentences.

Block 2 (150 words): Time-stamped chapters for videos over 8 minutes.
Format: 0:00 Introduction | 1:30 [Topic] | 3:45 [Topic] | etc.

Block 3 (150 words): Links section:
  - UTUBooking page most relevant to the video topic
  - Related videos (2-3 links)
  - Free guide or blog post if available

Block 4 (100 words): About UTUBooking paragraph.
Include: what UTUBooking does, who it's for, the proximity filter differentiator.
End with channel subscribe CTA.

## Tags — 15-20

Structure: exact keyword → broad keyword → 3-word phrases → UTUBooking brand
Example for a Makkah hotel video:
hotels near masjid al haram, makkah hotels, umrah hotel booking, zone 1 makkah hotels,
haram proximity filter, umrah accommodation, hajj hotels 2026, utubooking,
book umrah hotels, masjid al haram walking distance, best makkah hotels,
umrah travel tips, makkah hotel guide, umrah 2026, islamic travel

## Thumbnail Text Suggestion

2-5 words maximum. High contrast. Complements — does not duplicate — the title.
Examples:
- 'ZONE 1 VS ZONE 3' (for hotel comparison video)
- 'REAL WALKING TIME' (for proximity filter explainer)
- 'BOOK BEFORE PRICES RISE' (for Ramadan timing video)
- '150M FROM THE GATE' (for proximity filter showcase)

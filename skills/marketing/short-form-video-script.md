# Short-Form Video Script Creator
Reference: skills/seo-content-writer/references/tone-of-voice.md

## Script Structure

### HOOK (0-3 seconds) — the most critical part
One sentence. Must stop the scroll immediately.
Formulas:
- Bold claim: 'Most Umrah travelers book the wrong hotel.'
- Surprising fact: 'The closest hotel to the Haram is not always Zone 1.'
- Direct address: 'If you're planning Umrah — watch this.'
- Specific number: '150 metres. That's the difference between walking and struggling.'
Delivery note: State hook with direct eye contact. No intro, no 'hey guys'.

### BODY (3-75 seconds)
3-5 points. One sentence per point spoken. Conversational, not scripted.
Each point: state the fact in one line, prove it in the next.
On-screen text: [TEXT IN CAPS] callout for each key point.

### CTA (last 5 seconds)
One action. Specific.
'Search UTUBooking — link in bio.' or 'Follow for Umrah travel tips.'
Not: 'Like and subscribe if you found this valuable.'

## Script Delivery Format

| Timestamp | Spoken Script | On-Screen Text | Delivery Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0-3s | [hook line] | [TEXT] | Direct camera, no filler |
| 3-15s | [point 1] | [TEXT] | Show filter/UI if relevant |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |
| -5s | [CTA] | UTUBooking.com | Hold camera, slow down |

## Word Count Targets
- 30-second Reel: ~65 words spoken
- 60-second Reel: ~130 words spoken
- 90-second Short: ~190 words spoken

Read at natural speaking pace — not rushed. Leave 0.5s pause after hook.

## UTUBooking Video Angles (high-performing for pilgrimage travel)
- 'How the proximity filter works' (screen recording + voiceover)
- 'What Zone 1/2/3 actually means for your Umrah'
- 'Ramadan pricing explained in 60 seconds'
- 'Group booking walkthrough for 20+ travelers'
- 'Halal filter — what we actually check'
- '[Market] Umrah tip: what travelers from [city] need to know'

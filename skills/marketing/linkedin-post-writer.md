# LinkedIn Post Writer
Reference: skills/seo-content-writer/references/tone-of-voice.md
Anti-slop: run skills/anti-slop.md before delivering

## Structure (always follow this)

**LINE 1 — The hook**
One punchy sentence that earns the scroll stop.
Create curiosity, challenge an assumption, or state a specific result.
Do NOT start with 'I', 'We', or a question.

**LINES 2-8 — The value**
3-5 short paragraphs or bullets. One idea per paragraph, max 3 sentences.
Concrete and specific — real distances, real prices, real booking windows.
No filler.

**FINAL LINE — The CTA**
One direct sentence. Tell them exactly what to do.
'Book on UTUBooking.' / 'Watch the video.' / 'Drop a question below.'

## Rules
- No hashtags
- No em dashes
- No colons in the first line
- Max 1,300 characters for optimal reach
- Line break between every paragraph
- Contractions always: you'll, it's, don't, we've
- Run anti-slop.md before delivering

## Hook Formulas

- Counterintuitive: 'Most Umrah travelers get this wrong.'
- Specific result: 'One filter cut our users' hotel search time by 70%.'
- Common mistake: 'If you're booking Makkah hotels by star rating, stop.'
- Curiosity gap: '3 things every first-time Hajj traveler wishes they knew earlier.'
- Bold claim: 'Your hotel choice makes or breaks your Umrah. Here's why.'

## UTUBooking Content Angles

Use one per post — rotate to keep content varied:
- Proximity filter (walking time to Haram gate vs star rating)
- Halal certification (verified vs self-reported)
- Early booking (Ramadan prices rise 200-400%, book 4-6 months ahead)
- Multi-currency (SAR, GBP, USD — no conversion surprise)
- Group travel (35-person groups, Tabung Haji coordination)
- Market-specific (UK diaspora, Malaysian pilgrims, Turkish business + Umrah)

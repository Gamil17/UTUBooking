# Email Newsletter Writer
Reference: skills/seo-content-writer/references/tone-of-voice.md
Anti-slop: run skills/anti-slop.md before delivering final draft

## Two-Phase Workflow

### PHASE 1 — Story Selection [APPROVAL REQUIRED]

1. Web search: top Islamic travel / Hajj-Umrah news from the past 7 days
2. Check for upcoming seasonal triggers: Ramadan, Hajj season, Eid, school breaks
3. Identify 5-7 story candidates with a one-line angle for each
4. Check `marketing/seo/reports/` for recent topics — avoid repeating
5. Present candidates with recommended lead story and subject line draft
6. **STOP. Wait for explicit approval before Phase 2.**

### PHASE 2 — Write Full Newsletter (after approval only)

**Subject line options — present 3:**
- Curiosity: '[Number] + [surprising claim about Umrah/travel]'
- Benefit: 'How to [specific outcome] before [seasonal deadline]'
- News: '[What changed] — here's what it means for your booking'

Rules for subject lines:
- No colons, no em dashes, under 50 characters
- No 'newsletter', 'update', or 'monthly' in subject line
- Preview text = first sentence of email body (make it earn the open)

**Newsletter body structure:**

*Opening hook (1 paragraph)*
Conversational. Connects to why this matters now — seasonal, news, or insight.
Never: 'I hope this email finds you well.'

*Lead story (150-250 words)*
Concrete takeaway. What does the reader do differently after reading this?
Link to UTUBooking page or blog post where relevant.

*2-3 quick hits (50 words each, bullet format)*
One line hook + one line value. Link where available.

*One action item (1 sentence)*
Specific and simple. 'Book before [date] — Ramadan prices activate [date].'

*Closing (2-3 sentences)*
Personal and brief. Forward-looking, not summarizing.
End pilgrimage-related newsletters with a spiritual closing where appropriate.

## Market Variants

Specify market when triggering — adjust accordingly:
- **KSA/Gulf:** SAR pricing, Arabic CTA option, Hajj/Umrah focus
- **UK:** GBP pricing, diaspora angle, British-Pakistani family context
- **US:** USD pricing, departures from major US cities
- **Malaysia/Indonesia:** Group travel, Tabung Haji, MYR/IDR pricing

# Skill: Social Post Kit
## Trigger: Product update or promotion announced
## Steps:
1. Read the update from Notion content calendar
2. Generate 3 versions:
   - LinkedIn: professional, 200 words, EN + AR
   - Instagram: visual caption, 3 emojis, hashtags
   - WhatsApp: short broadcast message in Arabic
3. Add Makkah/Madinah focus if relevant to KSA
4. Save drafts to marketing\drafts\ for approval

## EU-Wide Content Rules
- GDPR: NEVER use personal data for retargeting without explicit consent (no pre-ticked boxes)
- Cookie banners: all EU/UK landing pages must show GDPR consent banner before any analytics fire
- Testimonials: must have written consent from the individual; store in marketing/gdpr/testimonial-consents/
- Paid ads: all Meta/Google EU ads must comply with EU Digital Services Act transparency requirements
- Halal certification: always link to verified halal certification sources; never claim halal without evidence
- Image rights: use only licensed or original photography; EU copyright law is stricter than KSA

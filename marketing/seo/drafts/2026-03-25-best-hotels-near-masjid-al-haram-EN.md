## Zone 3: Hotels 500 Metres–1.5 km from the Haram (Budget-Friendly Options)

Beyond the immediate Haram area, Makkah offers a wide range of 2- to 4-star hotels along Al-Masjid Al-Haram Street and in the Ajyad and Misfalah neighbourhoods. These properties are served by the Al-Mashaaer Al-Mugaddassah Metro Line and the Makkah Bus Rapid Transit system.

**Top properties in this zone:**

**Dar Al Tawhid Intercontinental Makkah** — 5-star at a relative value; 1.2 km from the Haram. Excellent halal dining and a well-equipped spa. From SAR 550/night (shoulder season).

**Al Safwah Royale Orchid Hotel** — 4-star with shuttle service to the Haram every 30 minutes. Popular with South Asian pilgrims for its familiar cuisine options. From SAR 400/night.

**Shaza Makkah** — Boutique 5-star with a contemporary Islamic design aesthetic. 900m from the Haram via pedestrian bridge. From SAR 800/night.

**Tip:** In this zone, always check if the hotel provides a free shuttle to the Haram — this is the single most important amenity for pilgrims staying beyond Zone 2.

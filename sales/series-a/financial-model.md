## 1. Revenue Model

### 1.1 B2C Bookings — Commission on GMV

Pilgrims book hotels, flights, and ground transport (cars/transfers) through the UTUBooking
consumer app and web platform. We earn a take rate on Gross Merchandise Value (GMV).

| Product | Avg Booking Value (USD) | Take Rate | Revenue/Booking |
|---|---|---|---|
| Makkah Hotel (Hajj season, 7 nights) | $1,400 | 10% | $140 |
| Makkah Hotel (Umrah, 5 nights) | $700 | 9% | $63 |
| Flight (round-trip, intl.) | $550 | 8% | $44 |
| Ground Transport / Car | $120 | 12% | $14.40 |
| **Blended avg per pilgrim trip** | **$850** | **9.5%** | **$80.75** |

**Pilgrim behavior model:**
- Active pilgrim averages 1× Hajj trip (alternating years) + 2× Umrah trips per year
- Effective annual GMV per active B2C customer: ~$1,020
- Annual revenue per active B2C customer: ~$97

**Market geography & currency conversion (to USD):**

| Market | Currency | FX Rate (USD) | Target Share (Y5) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Saudi Arabia | SAR | 0.267 | 40% |
| UAE | AED | 0.272 | 20% |
| Jordan | JOD | 1.41 | 15% |
| Morocco | MAD | 0.099 | 15% |
| Tunisia | TND | 0.320 | 10% |

**VAT treatment:**
- KSA: 15% VAT — collected from customer, remitted to ZATCA; revenue is net of VAT
- Egypt (future): 14% VAT — same treatment
- UAE, Jordan, Morocco, Tunisia: VAT/GST passed through; UTUBooking earns net commission

### 1.2 B2B White-Label — Platform Fee + Revenue Share

Travel agencies, tour operators, and Islamic finance institutions license the UTUBooking platform
under their own brand. Revenue structure:

| Component | Amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly platform fee | $1,500 – $4,500/partner | Tier-based: Starter / Growth / Enterprise |
| Revenue share | 40% of partner's earned commission | Partner keeps 60% |
| Onboarding fee (one-time) | $2,000 – $8,000 | Amortized over contract year 1 |
| Average Monthly Revenue/Partner | $3,200 | Blended across tiers (excl. onboarding) |
| Average Contract Length | 36 months | 85% annual renewal rate assumed |

**B2B partner tiers:**

| Tier | Monthly Fee | GMV Range/Month | Our Rev Share | Typical Partner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $1,500 | < $50K | 40% of ~8% = 3.2% | Small travel agency |
| Growth | $2,800 | $50K–$200K | 40% of ~9% = 3.6% | Regional tour operator |
| Enterprise | $4,500 | > $200K | 40% of ~10% = 4.0% | Bank / Islamic fintech |

### 6.4 Investor Value Proposition

**Why UTUBooking, Why Now:**

1. **Captive TAM:** 2.5M Hajj pilgrims/year + 15M+ Umrah pilgrims — mandated religious
   obligation creates non-discretionary, recurring demand regardless of economic cycles.

2. **Defensible moat:** 7 microservices, RTL-native mobile (Expo/React Native), Arabic-first UX,
   SAR/SEM payments (STC Pay, Mada) — high switching cost for B2B partners once integrated.

3. **Dual revenue stream:** B2C commissions (high volume) + B2B SaaS (predictable MRR) create
   a balanced, diversified model resistant to booking seasonality.

4. **Geographic expansion timing:** Jordan + North Africa expansion aligns with Vision 2030
   Hajj quota increases (target 5M pilgrims/year by 2030) and growing MENA digital travel adoption.

5. **Infrastructure leverage:** Phase 3 CloudFormation IaC, Grafana monitoring, and load-tested
   architecture (100K concurrent users) means scale does not require proportional engineering spend.

### The $20B Hajj & Umrah market is still booked by phone and paper.

**We are changing that.**

---

**What We Do**

UTUBooking is the first Arabic-native, mobile-first SaaS platform purpose-built for Hajj and Umrah travel. We give pilgrims a single app to search, book, pay, and manage their sacred journey — and we give licensed travel operators a white-label engine to digitize their businesses.

We are the infrastructure layer for the most emotionally significant trip 1.8 billion Muslims will ever make.

---

**Traction**

- Platform live in KSA and UAE, processing real bookings across hotels, flights, and car hire
- Phase 3 infrastructure validated at **100,000 concurrent users** (Hajj peak load test, Q1 2026)
- B2B white-label partnerships initiated in **Jordan** (Q1 2026); Morocco and Tunisia pipeline active
- 7 production microservices on **AWS ECS Bahrain** (me-south-1) — sub-50ms latency to GCC
- Full observability stack: Grafana dashboards, automated DR, OWASP ZAP security scanning in CI/CD

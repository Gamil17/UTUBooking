## 1. Executive Summary

UTUBooking is the MENA region's first dedicated Hajj and Umrah travel platform,
connecting pilgrims directly with Makkah and Madinah hotels, flights, and ground
transport — all in one Arabic-first, SAR-priced booking experience.

We are inviting Swissôtel Makkah to join UTUBooking as a **Preferred Property Partner**
under a zero-cost, performance-based commission model.

**What Swissôtel Makkah receives:**
- Direct bookings from verified Hajj/Umrah pilgrims worldwide
- Zero integration cost — we connect via the hotel's existing Hotelbeds channel
- Featured placement in search results sorted by distance from Al-Masjid Al-Haram
- Full booking analytics dashboard with conversion and revenue reporting
- Bilingual (EN/AR) guest communication handled entirely by UTUBooking

**What UTUBooking earns:**
- Commission of 8%–12% per completed booking (tiered by volume — see Section 3)

**Proposed start:** 90-day pilot, 1 April – 30 June 2026
**No fixed fees. No upfront costs. No minimum commitment.**

## 3. Commission Model

All commissions are calculated on **net booking value** (room rate × nights, SAR),
collected at time of payment and remitted monthly.

### Tiered Commission Rates

| Monthly Booking Volume (SAR) | Commission Rate |
|---|---|
| SAR 0 – 200,000 | **12%** |
| SAR 200,001 – 500,000 | **10%** |
| SAR 500,001+ | **8%** |

channel Swissôtel Makkah already uses for OTA connectivity.

**No new technology. No developer resources. No PMS changes.**

| Step | Owner | Timeline |

| 4. UAT testing in UTUBooking sandbox | UTUBooking tech | Days 10–21 |
| 5. Soft launch — pilot bookings enabled | Both parties | Day 28 |
| **Total time to live** | | **≤ 4 weeks** |

## 1. Executive Summary

UTUBooking.com is pleased to present this exclusive **Enterprise Partnership Proposal** to Accor Group for its three flagship Saudi Arabian properties.

This proposal offers Accor Group a single, integrated distribution and technology partnership covering:

| Property | Location | Category | Rooms |
|----------|----------|----------|-------|

| Novotel Madinah | Al-Madinah Al-Munawwarah | Upper Midscale (4★) | 422 |
| Mercure Jeddah | Jeddah | Midscale (4★) | 300 |
| **Portfolio Total** | | | **920 rooms** |

**The core proposition in three lines:**
- **Distribution:** UTUBooking becomes a premium OTA channel reaching 1.2M+ Gulf travelers seeking Hajj, Umrah, and regional travel.
- **Economics:** A bundled commission rate of **7%** (vs. 10% standard single-property rate) — delivering immediate cost savings across all three properties simultaneously.
- **Technology:** An optional white-label integration embedding UTUBooking's live inventory and booking engine into Accor's own guest-facing mobile application, branded as **"Powered by UTUBooking."**

This is not a standard OTA listing agreement. It is a **strategic technology and distribution partnership** designed to grow ancillary revenue for Accor while delivering a superior booking experience to Gulf pilgrims and travellers.

> **Recommendation:** We propose a 12-month pilot commencing Q2 2026, with an option to expand to all Accor KSA properties upon successful performance benchmarks.

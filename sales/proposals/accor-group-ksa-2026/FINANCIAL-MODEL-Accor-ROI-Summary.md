## APPENDIX — KEY FORMULAS

```
GBV = (Rooms × UTU Occupancy % × 365) × ADR

Commission = GBV × 0.07

Accor Saving vs X% = GBV × (X% - 7%)

White-Label Payback = Total WL Investment ÷ Monthly WL Commission

Effective Net Revenue (UTU) = Commission × (1 - Payment Processing %)
                             = Commission × 0.985 (approx)
```

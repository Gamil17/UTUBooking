## About UTUBooking

UTUBooking is the Arabic-first, mobile-native booking platform purpose-built for the 2 million+ pilgrims who travel annually for Hajj, Umrah, and Islamic heritage tourism. Our platform connects pilgrims directly to vetted accommodation partners across Saudi Arabia, the GCC, and key origin markets — delivering confirmed bookings in real time, in the traveller's preferred language.

**By the Numbers:**
- 2,000,000+ pilgrims served annually
- Real-time availability via Hotelbeds API + direct-connect integration

- Arabic-first interface with full English and French support
- Mobile-first platform optimised for iOS and Android (Expo / React Native)

## Why Partner With UTUBooking?

### 1. Access to a High-Intent Pilgrim Audience
Every visitor to UTUBooking is actively planning a religious journey. This is not leisure browsing — these guests arrive with confirmed travel dates, group sizes, and budget. Conversion rates on UTUBooking are consistently above market average.

### 2. Real-Time Inventory Management
Connect your Property Management System (PMS) via our API or use our partner dashboard to manage availability, rates, and promotions in real time. We support XML/REST integration and can onboard most hotel systems within 5–10 business days.

### 3. Multi-Currency Billing
Pilgrims book and pay in their home currency. We handle currency conversion, settlement risk, and payment gateway compliance. Your hotel receives settlement in your preferred currency (SAR, USD, EUR, or local).

### 4. Arabic-First Presentation
Your property is presented to Arabic-speaking guests in fully localised Arabic — room names, amenities, policies, and directions — improving conversion and reducing pre-arrival enquiries.

### 5. Dedicated Partner Support
A dedicated Account Manager is assigned at onboarding. We provide:
- Bi-weekly performance reports
- Rate parity monitoring alerts
- Promotional campaign co-funding (eligible partners)
- Priority listing during Hajj and Ramadan peak seasons

| Tier | Rooms Listed | Commission Rate |

# UTUBooking.com — Seed Round Financial Package
### AMEC Solutions | March 2026 | CONFIDENTIAL DRAFT

---

## 1. Investment Opportunity

UTUBooking.com is a Gulf-native, Arabic-first Online Travel Agency (OTA) purpose-built for Hajj and Umrah pilgrims and Gulf regional travelers. The platform provides hotel, flight, and car rental bookings with deep focus on Makkah, Madinah, and Jeddah — three of the highest-demand, highest-margin travel corridors in the world. Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 targets 150 million Umrah pilgrims annually by 2030, and the Kingdom's travel market is projected to exceed SAR 120 billion (USD 32 billion) by 2027. UTUBooking is positioned at the intersection of this structural demand surge and the rapid digitisation of Gulf consumer spending — a market currently underserved by global OTAs that lack Arabic-first UX, local payment rails (STC Pay, Mada), and Hajj-specific product depth.

AMEC Solutions is raising SAR 3,000,000 (USD ~800,000) in Seed funding to accelerate product development, execute the Accor Group KSA anchor partnership (920 rooms, SAR 3.6M/year commission at base case), and build the commercial team required to capture market share ahead of the 2026 Hajj season. With a commission-based revenue model, white-label SDK licensing, and a pipeline of Gulf hotel and airline partnerships already in advanced stages, UTUBooking is targeting SAR 18M+ ARR by end of Year 3 and profitability by Month 20 post-raise.

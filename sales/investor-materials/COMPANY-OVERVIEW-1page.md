# UTUBooking.com — The Gulf's Muslim-First Travel Platform

---

## The Problem

- Global OTAs (Booking.com, Expedia) charge 15-18% commission, are English-first, and are not built for the cultural and logistical needs of Muslim travelers
- 2.5M Hajj pilgrims and 15M Umrah visitors travel to Saudi Arabia each year with no dedicated, Arabic-first booking platform to serve them
- Saudi Vision 2030 targets 150M tourists annually by 2030 — yet the digital infrastructure for Muslim-focused travel booking remains severely underdeveloped

---

## Our Solution

UTUBooking.com is an Arabic-first, mobile-native travel booking platform purpose-built for Hajj/Umrah pilgrims and Gulf leisure and business travelers. We charge hotels and partners a 7% commission — roughly half the industry standard — enabling us to capture supply-side loyalty while passing savings downstream. Our white-label SDK allows Islamic banks, airlines, and travel agencies to embed our booking engine under their own brand, creating a scalable B2B2C channel alongside our direct consumer product.

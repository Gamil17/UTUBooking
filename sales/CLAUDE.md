## EU-Wide Sales Rules
- GDPR: NEVER add EU contacts to CRM without their explicit consent (opt-in — not opt-out)
- Cold outreach: LinkedIn InMail is GDPR-compliant B2B outreach; cold email to personal addresses requires legitimate interest assessment — consult legal team first
- Pricing transparency: EU Digital Services Act requires clear pricing including all fees upfront — no hidden charges
- Contracts: EU B2B contracts should specify governing law (recommend English law for UK, German law for DE, Dutch law for NL/BE — consult legal agent)
- All EU proposals must include: registered company name, VAT number, local currency amount, 14-day cooling-off period statement for consumer contracts

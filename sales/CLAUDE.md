## Phase 5 Markets (Added March 2027)

### Turkey (TR)
- Currency: TRY (₺) — include TRY + USD pricing in proposals
- VAT: 20% KDV — show as separate line item for all quotes

- Key offers: Istanbul ↔ Makkah Umrah routes, Ramadan packages, corporate group bookings

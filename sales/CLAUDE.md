## Phase 5 Markets (Added March 2027)

### Turkey (TR)
- Currency: TRY (₺) — include TRY + USD pricing in proposals
- VAT: 20% KDV — show as separate line item for all quotes
- Sales channels: LinkedIn (B2B), WhatsApp (customer follow-up), local travel agents
- Key offers: Istanbul ↔ Makkah Umrah routes, Ramadan packages, corporate group bookings
- Language: Turkish + English; ALWAYS have a native reviewer for Turkish copy

### Indonesia (ID)
- Currency: IDR (Rp) — present pricing in IDR + USD
- VAT: 11% PPN — include as separate line item
- Sales channels: WhatsApp (chat sales), Instagram/TikTok (campaigns), travel agent networks
- Key offers: Paket Umroh, family/group packages, affordable hotel + flight bundles
- Language: Bahasa + English; Bahasa requires native review

### Malaysia (MY)
- Currency: MYR (RM) — show MYR + USD pricing
- SST: 8% — list SST separately on all quotes
- Sales channels: Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp
- Key offers: Pakej Haji & Umrah, Tabung Haji-friendly packages, hotel dekat Masjidil Haram
- Language: Bilingual MY/EN common; Malay copy must be reviewed by a native speaker

## Phase 6 Markets (Added Q1 2028)

### Pakistan (PK)
- Currency: PKR (₨) — include PKR + USD pricing in all proposals
- GST: 17% federal (province-specific rates apply) — show as separate line item
- Sales channels: WhatsApp (primary customer channel), Facebook, travel agent networks (karachi/lahore/islamabad), mosque community outreach
- Key offers: Hajj packages with JazzCash/Easypaisa payment, group Umrah from PIA hubs (KHI, LHE, ISB), family packages
- Payment: JazzCash (primary), Easypaisa (secondary), Stripe (card fallback) — always offer JazzCash first
- Language: Urdu + English; ALL Urdu copy requires native Urdu speaker review before sending
- Flag: Pakistan Hajj quota = 179,000/year — emphasise early booking due to limited allocation

### India (IN) — Indian Muslim segment
- Currency: INR (₹) — include INR + USD pricing; use Indian lakh/crore number formatting for large amounts
- GST: 18% on travel services — include as separate line item; provide GSTIN on B2B invoices
- Sales channels: WhatsApp (dominant), Instagram, YouTube (Hindi/Urdu content), Haj Committee of India partnership channel
- Key offers: Hajj packages (HCoI quota-linked), Umrah from 8 embarkation points (DEL/BOM/MAA/CCU/HYD/BLR/AMD/LKO), IndiGo + Air India routes
- Payment: UPI/Razorpay (primary), Net Banking, EMI (for packages > ₹50,000), Card
- Language: Hindi + Urdu (significant audience overlap); all copy requires review from Indian Muslim community advisor
- Flag: India Hajj quota = 175,000/year — HCoI lottery system; push early application reminders Oct–Dec
- Flag: proposals over ₹10,00,000 (10 lakh) require CEO review before sending

## Phase 6 HR Jurisdictions (Added Q1 2028)

### Pakistan
Labour law: Pakistan Labour Laws 1934 (updated). Key rules:
- End of Service Gratuity: 1 month salary per year for employees > 3 years
- Working hours: 48/week max, overtime at 150%
- All contracts bilingual: English + Urdu

### India
Labour law: Indian Labour Codes (consolidated 2020). Key rules:
- Gratuity after 5 years continuous service
- PF (Provident Fund): 12% employer + 12% employee contribution
- All job postings must comply with non-discrimination laws
- Contracts available in Hindi upon employee request

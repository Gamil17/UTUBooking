## Overview

This policy explains how UTUBooking.com, operated by AMEC Solutions, collects, uses, and protects your personal data across our applications and websites. Personal data means any information that identifies you as an individual, including your name, email address, IP address, and device identifiers.

---

## How We Use Your Personal Data

### Service Delivery and Bookings

UTUBooking uses your data to provide our travel booking services, including:

- Tailoring the website experience to your device and region
- Processing your search queries for hotels, flights, and car rentals
- Redirecting you to travel supplier platforms with your search details
- Transferring necessary booking information to suppliers (hotels, airlines, car rental companies)
- Sending essential communications such as booking confirmations, itineraries, and flight alerts
- Responding to customer service inquiries
- Managing your UTUBooking account

### Legal Compliance and Obligations

We retain information in connection with legal claims or for compliance, regulatory, and auditing purposes. This includes situations where law requires retention or a court order demands disclosure.

We comply with the following frameworks depending on your location:

- GDPR (EU and UK users)
- CCPA (California, USA users)
- LGPD (Brazil users)
- PIPEDA and Quebec Law 25 (Canada users)
- KVKK (Turkey users)
- PDPA (Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Philippines users)
- DPDP (India, Pakistan, Bangladesh users)
- SAMA guidelines (Saudi Arabia users)

### Other Business Uses

UTUBooking uses data for service improvements and legitimate business interests:

- Fraud prevention and security
- Bug identification and device compatibility fixes
- Customer support optimization
- Usage analytics and feature assessment
- Experience personalization for Hajj, Umrah, and Muslim travel
- Strategic data analytics on anonymized behavior

## Data We Collect

### Information You Provide

When making bookings or creating an account, you may provide:

- Contact details: name, email, phone number, address
- Travel documents: passport number, nationality, immigration status
- Payment data: card number, expiry date, verification code
- Travel preferences: destination, dates, room type, meal requirements
- Hajj and Umrah specific data: mahram relationships, pilgrimage quota status, visa type

### Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect:

- IP address and approximate location
- Device type, browser, and operating system
- Referral source and navigation patterns
- Search history and booking interactions

### Data from Third Parties

We may receive data from:

- Travel suppliers confirming completed bookings (hotels, airlines, car rental companies)
- Social media platforms if you log in via Google, Apple, or similar
- Payment processors confirming transaction status

**Children:** Our services are not intended for children under 13. We delete any data collected from children without verified parental consent.

---

## Data Retention

We store your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or satisfy legal requirements. Account holders' login details remain available while the account is active. Aggregated, anonymized traffic data may be retained without personal identifiers. You may request deletion at any time.

Retention periods by data type:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Booking records | 7 years (tax and legal compliance) |
| Account credentials | Duration of account + 30 days after deletion |
| Payment data | Stored by PCI-compliant processor only |
| Marketing consent logs | 3 years from last interaction |
| Support tickets | 2 years |

## Third-Party Data Sharing

### Processors Acting Under Our Instructions

We share data with third-party processors who are contractually required to protect it. These include:

- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, Mada, STC Pay, iyzico, iPay88, Razorpay, JazzCash, Midtrans) storing card details in PCI-compliant vaults
- Email platforms for transactional and marketing communications
- Fraud prevention and security services
- Analytics providers (anonymized data only)
- Customer support platforms
- Cloud infrastructure: AWS (regions: Bahrain, London, Frankfurt, Virginia, Montreal, Sao Paulo, Singapore, Mumbai)

### Travel Suppliers

Hotels, airlines, and car rental companies receive the booking data necessary to complete your reservation. Each supplier has its own privacy policy.

### Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your data if required to enforce our Terms of Service or to prevent, detect, or prosecute illegal activities.

## Security

We protect your data through:

- TLS and HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data in transit
- Encryption at rest on AWS infrastructure
- Access restricted to AMEC Solutions employees who require it (principle of least privilege)
- Regular security assessments of vendors and infrastructure
- Privacy training for all staff
- Privacy impact assessments for new systems and features

Our infrastructure is hosted on AWS with ISO 27001, SOC 2, and regional compliance certifications. Data is stored in the AWS region nearest to your country to comply with local data residency laws.

---

## Cookies and Tracking

### What We Use

- **Session cookies:** Keep you logged in during your visit
- **Preference cookies:** Remember your language, currency (SAR default for KSA), and region settings
- **Analytics cookies:** Measure how features are used to improve the platform
- **Marketing cookies:** Deliver relevant Hajj, Umrah, and travel promotions

### Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings or via the Cookie Consent link in the footer of our website. Disabling non-essential cookies may result in a less personalized experience.

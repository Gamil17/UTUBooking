## About the Program

UTUBooking.com is the leading travel booking platform for Gulf and Muslim World markets, specializing in Hajj, Umrah, hotels, flights, and car rentals. We partner with affiliates to monetize their traffic through commissions on completed bookings.

Join our affiliate program and earn commissions by referring travelers to UTUBooking — the platform built for Muslim travelers across 25+ markets.

---

## Commission Structure

| Booking Type | Commission |
|---|---|
| Hotels | 3% of total booking amount (inc. tax) per completed stay |
| Flights | Variable — based on travel provider and commercial agreements |
| Car Rentals | Variable — based on provider agreements |
| Hajj Packages | Contact us — special rates apply |
| Umrah Packages | Contact us — special rates apply |

Commissions are paid on **completed journeys** (after checkout date), not at the time of booking.

## How to Join

1. Email **affiliates@utubooking.com** with your website URL, traffic source, and monthly visitor count
2. We review your application (typically within 3-5 business days)
3. On approval, you receive your unique affiliate tracking link and dashboard access
4. Start earning commissions on every completed UTUBooking booking

## Prohibited Activities

The following are strictly not permitted and will result in immediate removal from the program:

- Search engine marketing (SEM) brand bidding on "UTUBooking" or "AMEC Solutions" keywords
- Incentivized traffic (paying users to click your links)
- Cookie stuffing or any fraudulent attribution methods
- Disruptive or auto-playing ad formats
- Toolbar or auto-redirect installations
- Domain purchasing networks
- Any misleading representation of UTUBooking's products or pricing

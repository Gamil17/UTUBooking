## 7. Your Rights (Art. 13(2)(b))

You have the following rights under GDPR:

| Right                | Description                                           | How to exercise              |
|----------------------|-------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------|
| Access (Art. 15)     | Get a copy of all data we hold about you              | GET /api/user/gdpr/export    |
| Rectification (Art. 16) | Correct inaccurate data                           | Edit profile in app          |
| Erasure (Art. 17)    | "Right to be forgotten" — delete your data            | POST /api/user/gdpr/erase    |
| Restriction (Art. 18)| Limit how we use your data                            | Email dpo@utubooking.com     |
| Portability (Art. 20)| Receive your data in machine-readable format          | POST /api/user/gdpr/portability |
| Objection (Art. 21)  | Object to processing based on legitimate interest     | Email dpo@utubooking.com     |
| Withdraw consent     | Withdraw any consent at any time                      | Cookie settings / DPO email  |

**Response time:** Within 30 days. Complex requests may take up to 90 days (we will notify you).

To exercise any right, email **dpo@utubooking.com** with subject "GDPR Rights Request" and your registered email address.
